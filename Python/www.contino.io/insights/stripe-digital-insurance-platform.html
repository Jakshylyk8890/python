Contino and Stripe: 3 Fresh Takes on Why You Should Modernise Your Digital Insurance Platform

Contino is proud to be an official strategic partner with Stripe, a leading financial infrastructure platform.

As part of the Stripe Partner Ecosystem, Contino can help businesses get up and running with Stripes fully integrated suite of payments and financial services solutions quickly and easily.

In this infographic, we highlight three unique takes on why it's time to fully embrace a native digital insurance platform for your business and customers:


1. Create cohesive and frictionless online customer journeys

A smooth customer journey can make all the difference when it comes to overall customer satisfaction. By leveraging third party products like Stripe to simplify your checkout process, you can take advantage of a myriad of modern payment methods such as Apple / Google paya great way to increase customer retention.

Using such plug-and-play functionality allows your core development and business teams to spend most of their effort on other modernisation efforts. To choose the correct project to implement frictionless payments, Contino has developed our Lighthouse approach for a culture of iterative experimentation, fast feedback and scaling transformation.

2. Gain actionable insights from online customer behaviour

In the UK, malicious fraudulent transactions currently cost the insurance industry over 100m per year. At the heart of a new digital platform should be a strategy on how best to leverage dataeither generated by your customers, captured by external systems, or curated by internal teams.

A truly modern digital platform should treat data as its most valuable asset. With Continos experience of working with large heavily regulated enterprise organisations, we can be your trusted advisor in designing data platforms to support your analytical and operational data decision making abilities. By leveraging machine learning tools like Radar For Fraud Teams from Stripe, you are empowered to define your risk profile for transactions and feed this data into your wider data platformto help improve visibility and insight into customer buying habits.

3. Automate everything and reduce manual errors

Distributing funds to different business areas, franchises and divisions is often a manual process. When we consider distributing funds internationally, businesses also have to consider domestic tax regulation and transaction fees to bank accounts. This further complicates reconciliation and distribution flowsadding the real chance of delays and errors into your processes.

Using Stripes native support for cross-regional payouts, insurers can now automatically flow funds to the right business units across your organisation and put an end to the manual reconciliation processes.

Contino is proud to be a partner of Stripe with a number of certified architects and developers. If you'd like help integrating Stripe into your digital platform please feel free to get in touch!
